# IoT Sensor Knowledge Base
# Describes sensor types, reading ranges, normal thresholds, and interpretation guidelines.

## Motion Sensor
type: motion_sensor
technology: PIR (Passive Infrared)
output: binary (motion_detected: true/false)
detection_range: 6 metres, 120° angle
response_time: < 1 second
cooldown_period: 30 seconds after last motion
normal_behaviour: Triggers when a person moves within range; resets after cooldown.
use_cases: occupancy detection, automatic lighting, security alerts

## Temperature Sensor
type: temperature_sensor
technology: NTC thermistor / digital I2C
unit: Celsius (°C)
range: -10°C to 60°C
accuracy: ±0.5°C
normal_indoor_range: 18°C – 26°C
comfort_range: 20°C – 24°C
alert_threshold_high: > 30°C (possible overheating)
alert_threshold_low: < 14°C (too cold, possible heating malfunction)
use_cases: thermostat control, HVAC automation, anomaly detection

## Humidity Sensor
type: humidity_sensor
technology: capacitive
unit: Percent Relative Humidity (%RH)
range: 0% – 100%
accuracy: ±3%RH
normal_indoor_range: 30% – 60%
alert_threshold_high: > 70% (mould risk)
alert_threshold_low: < 25% (too dry, discomfort)
use_cases: HVAC control, comfort optimisation, mould prevention

## CO2 Sensor
type: co2_sensor
technology: NDIR (Non-Dispersive Infrared)
unit: ppm (parts per million)
outdoor_baseline: ~420 ppm
good_air_quality: < 800 ppm
moderate_air_quality: 800 – 1200 ppm
poor_air_quality: > 1200 ppm (ventilation recommended)
hazardous: > 2000 ppm (open windows immediately)
use_cases: ventilation control, air quality alerts, occupancy estimation

## Air Quality Index (AQI)
type: aqi_sensor
technology: electrochemical / optical particle counter
unit: AQI (0–500 scale)
good: 0 – 50
moderate: 51 – 100
unhealthy_for_sensitive: 101 – 150
unhealthy: 151 – 200
very_unhealthy: 201 – 300
hazardous: > 300
use_cases: air purifier control, ventilation alerts, health monitoring

## Smart Light (Actuator)
type: smart_light
technology: LED with PWM dimming
brightness_unit: percentage (0–100%)
color_temperature_unit: Kelvin (K)
warm_white: 2700K – 3000K (relaxation, evening)
neutral_white: 3500K – 4500K (task work)
cool_white: 5000K – 6500K (focus, morning)
energy_consumption: 8W – 15W typical
use_cases: scene control, circadian rhythm support, occupancy-based automation

## Thermostat (Actuator)
type: thermostat
technology: digital relay control + PID
setpoint_range: 16°C – 30°C
modes: heat, cool, auto, off
auto_mode_behaviour: Switches between heat and cool to maintain setpoint within ±0.5°C.
schedule_support: yes (7-day programmable)
use_cases: energy saving, comfort scheduling, temperature-event reactions

## Smart Lock
type: smart_lock
technology: motorised deadbolt + BLE/Z-Wave
states: locked, unlocked
auto_lock_delay: configurable (30s – 10 min)
access_methods: PIN code, mobile app, voice command, auto-unlock on proximity
audit_log: yes (timestamp + method for each event)
use_cases: security, access control, guest management

## Smart Plug
type: smart_plug
technology: relay + energy metering chip (e.g. ADE7953)
energy_monitoring: real-time power (W), cumulative energy (kWh)
max_load: 2300W (10A at 230V)
use_cases: appliance control, energy monitoring, overload protection

## Smart Blind
type: smart_blind
technology: tubular motor + hall-effect encoder
position_unit: percentage (0% = fully closed, 100% = fully open)
tilt_unit: percentage (0% = horizontal, 100% = vertical)
use_cases: solar shading, privacy control, glare reduction, circadian lighting
